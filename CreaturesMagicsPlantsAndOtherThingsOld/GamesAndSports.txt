[[BOARD GAMES]]

Rægatafl:
	Origin:   Oddarsveldi
	Factions: Factions are usually Trœkkar (Trolls) colored with brown, and Alfar (Elves) colored with green.

	Workings:
		Rægatafl is basically Xiangqi, but the pieces are replaced with:
			- 砲, 炮 is replaced with Northmannish rune G  (gláttokr)
			- 兵, 卒 is replaced with Northmannish rune S  (svérkr)
			- 車, 俥 is replaced with Northmannish rune F  (fekíg)
			- 馬, 傌 is replaced with Northmannish rune H  (hreispr)
			- 士, 仕 is replaced with Northmannish rune L  (lókmandr)
			- 象, 相 is replaced with Northmannish rune B  (byr)
			- 將, 帥 is replaced with Northmannish rune K (konungr)
