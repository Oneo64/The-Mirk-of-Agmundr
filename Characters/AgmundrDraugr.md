Agmundr was a Viking explorer who discovered Agmundarland. During his stay there, he was struck by an arrow amidst a battle with the natives of the island and was buried in a mound.

## Appearance
Agmundr is a tall, withered corpse, with greyed eyes and sparse black hair. Its face is old, wrinkled, and bony, and its skin is dark and greyish green, a result of idling inside a cold, dry tomb for decades. There is a gnarly wound in its stomach, as if someone drove a sharp object through it.

Agmundr wears a suit of old maille and heavy, ragged clothes. On its head sits a rusty helmet. It carries a longseax and a rotten shield.

## Personality
Agmundr is extremely playful yet cruel, having no regard for any traditions it used to have. Like many draugr, it is extremely and violently protective of its valuables.

## Abilities
Unless disturbed, Agmundr sits at its burial spot with absolute stillness. If someone touches any of its equipment, it awakens and rises from its burial spot.

### Physicality
Agmundr is particularly skilled with the longseax. It is twice as strong as a normal living man.

### Draugric powers
Because Agmundr is a draugr, it has a few abilities that only they can have. It possesses the ability to possess creatures, and killing the creature doesn't kill the draugr. It is also able to turn itself into a billow of mist or disguise itself as a deer, elk, or moose. It can also give dreams and visions to other people.
