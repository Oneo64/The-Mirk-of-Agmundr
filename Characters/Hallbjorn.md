Hallbjörn the Uncoverer was a Viking explorer who led a group of settlers to Agmundarland, establishing a settlement there. He is now a chieftain, overseeing his place with pride.

## Appearance
Hallbjörn has a lean body and is around 5'9" in height. His hair is black with grey streaks, paired with a full, scruffy beard. His green eyes are deep-set and have a naturally withered shape, giving him a scary appearance.

Hallbjörn wears a dark cloak, a muted red tunic, dark pants, a ring belt, and a pair of shoes. When in combat, he wears a maille hauberk and spectacle helmet with a maille mask.

## Personality
Hallbjörn is arrogant, rowdy, and talkative. He is a little stubborn and can get argumentative, usually defending anything he believes until he is directly proven wrong.

As a great explorer, Hallbjörn is very adventurous, wanting to see something for himself rather than merely listening to stories of it. Because of his explorative personality, he is willing to join any adventuring or exploring party just to experience the "old stories".

However, Hallbjörn also recognises his duty as a leader and must restrain himself from going on expedition parties.

## Abilities
Hallbjörn is intimidating as a warrior, fighting his enemies with a Dane axe. He usually takes a defensive stance when in combat, taunting his foes to strike if they cower.
