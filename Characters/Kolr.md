# Kolr
Kolr was the local blacksmith of Álahringr. He didn't have a particularly strong relationship with anyone except for Ljörn, a local boy who would sneak into his workplace whenever he came off work. He had always suspected someone was going through his things, but he could never catch them.

Unfortunately, on one fateful night, a Viking raid happened in the village, and Kolr's house burned down. He was never found again.
